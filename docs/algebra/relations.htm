Condiditions on Ordered Pairs

Introduction

Suppose we have two sets, A and B. The cartesian product relates all elements ai from A to all elements bj from B. This creates a set R of ordered pairs (a, b):

A⨯B={(a,b)|a∈A∧b∈B}
