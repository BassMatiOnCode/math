Intercept Theorem 1 of Thales

Let a,b be two lines intersecting in the point O=A0=B0, which are crossed by a set of parallel lines AiBi according to figure 1. Then the ratio of two line segments on line a equals the ratio of the corresponding line segments on line b:

AiAj AmAn = BiBj BmBn

with i,j,m,n∈ℤ.

The converse of this theorem is also true: If The ratio of line segments on line a equals the ratio of line segments on line b, then the crossing lines are parallel.
