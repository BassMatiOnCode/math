A Never Ending Journey

Introduction

Following a the video How to self study pure math by Aleph 0, the following roadmap is suggested:

Linear Algebra

Linear Algebra is about vector spaces and the maps between them.

Literature:

Sheldon Axler - Linear Algebra Done Right

Emphasis on examples, which makes it easy to follow.

Published a paper Down with Determinants

He also has a Youtube playlist.

Adrian Banner - Linear Algebra

Video playlist of lectures at Princeton University.

University of Waterloo - Linear Algebra 1

Online course

Special player which stops for exercises.

Andrews University - Advanced Linear Algebra

Playlist

Filmed lectures. Very understandable.

Real Analysis

This is calculus, rigurously, from a proofs perspective. Literature:

Stephen Abbott - Understanding Analysis

Accompanied by a video playlist by Francis Su.

Point Set Topology

This is about taking concepts from real analysis and linear algebra, and putting them in a more abstract context.

University of Toronto - MAT327

Online. Extensive resources.

Complex Analysis

This is calculus with complex numbers.

Elias Wegert - Visual Complex Functions

An introduction with face portraits. Perfect introduction before getting into the details

Serge Lang - Comples Analysis

Many illustrations.

Petra Bonfert-Taylor - Analysis of a Complex Kind

Youtube playlist

Group Theory

The study of symmetry.

Benedict Gross - Abstract Algebra

Video playlist

I. N. Herstein - Topics in Algebra

Chapter 2, Group Theory. Lots of examples and exercises.

Galois Theory

Applied group theory.

Aleph 0 - Introduction to Galois Theory

Youtube

Tom Leinster - Galois Theory

Lecture notes, University of Edinbugh, 2022...2023, provide in-depth explanations.

Differential Geometry

The study of curved surfaces in higher-dimensional space, called "manifolds".

William M. Boothby - An Introduction to Differentiable Manifolds and Reimannian Geometry.

Aleph 0 - Video

youtube

Algebraic Topology

How to use methods from group theory to study questions from topology.

Allen Hatcher - Algebraic Topology

Tons of pictures and excercises.

Pierre Albin - Algebraic Topology

Playlist. Follows the book closely.
