Numbers: A Concise Review

Natural Numbers

The set of natural or counting numbers is the starting point of all the different kinds of numbers we will encounter.

The natural numbers have no associated arithmetic operations defined beyond counting and the addition of numbers. Especially, the subtraction cannot be defined on the natural numbers.
