A field of numbers is a system of objects, called numbers, combined with the operations of addition, subtraction, multiplication and division that satisfies the usual arighmetic laws.

In particular, it must be possioble to subtract andy number from any other, and divide any number by a non–zero number to another such number.

The associative, commutative, identity and inverse laws must hold for each addition and multiplication. The distributive law must hold for multiplication over addition.
